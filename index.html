Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our internet services

What internet speeds do you offer?

We offer various speed tiers ranging from 100 Mbps to 1 Gbps, designed to meet different household and business needs. All plans include unlimited data usage.

How much does installation cost?

Professional installation is free for all Home Basic and Home Pro plans. Our certified technicians will set up your equipment and ensure optimal performance at no extra charge.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No, we believe in earning your business every month. All our plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts or early termination fees.

What kind of customer support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 technical support through multiple channels including chat, email, and online resources. Business Elite customers receive priority support with dedicated account managers.

Is fiber internet available in my area?

We're rapidly expanding our fiber network across the country. Contact us with your address to check availability and learn about upcoming service areas in your region.
